Chicken Spaghetti Carbonara

from PxHere

Spaghetti Carbonara (chicken) is a rich and comforting pasta dish. Tender chicken pieces are tossed with spaghetti in a silky sauce made from eggs, cheese, and black pepper. With simple ingredients and quick preparation, it’s a perfect weeknight meal that feels indulgent.

How To Make Chicken Spaghetti Carbonara

You can follow this simple recipe to make a creamy Chicken Spaghetti Carbonara at home.

Ingredients

  • Pasta: 400 g (14 oz) spaghetti.
  • Chicken: 200 g (7 oz) chicken breast or thigh, diced into small pieces.
  • Eggs: 4 large yolks + 1 whole egg.
  • Pepper: Freshly cracked black pepper.
  • Salt: For pasta water.
  • Olive oil: 1–2 Tbsp.

How to Make Chicken Spaghetti Carbonara Step-By-Step

  1. Preheat & Prep
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    • In a bowl, whisk 4 yolks + 1 whole egg with grated cheese and a generous pinch of black pepper until smooth and creamy. Set aside.
  2. Cook the pasta
    • Add spaghetti to the boiling water and cook until al dente (8–9 minutes).
    • Reserve 1 cup pasta water, then drain.
  3. Cook the chicken
    • In a skillet over medium heat, warm olive oil.
    • Add chicken pieces and sauté for 6–8 minutes until golden brown and cooked through.
    • Remove the skillet from the heat.
  4. Combine pasta and chicken
    • Add the drained pasta to the skillet with the chicken. Toss well so the pasta is coated with oil and juices.
    • Let it cool for 30 seconds off the heat.
  5. Make the sauce
    • Pour in the egg-cheese mixture, tossing quickly and continuously.
    • Add splashes of reserved pasta water as needed until a glossy, creamy sauce coats the pasta.
  6. Finish & serve
    • Season with extra black pepper.
    • Sprinkle with more cheese on top.
    • Serve immediately while warm and creamy.
