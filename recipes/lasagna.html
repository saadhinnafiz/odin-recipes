Lasagna

Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food, layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy cheese, and a golden top fresh from the oven. It’s the kind of dish that fills the whole kitchen with a mouth watering aroma and brings everyone running to the table.

Credit: PoiseWinsTitles, May 2011

How To Make Lasagna

You can follow along this simple recipe to make a delicious Lasagna at home

Ingridients

  • Meat: A hearty mix of sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef gives this lasagna its rich, meaty flavor.
  • Onion and garlic: Chopped onion and a couple of garlic cloves are sautéed with the meat to add depth and aroma.
  • Tomato base: The sauce comes together with crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste for a full-bodied taste.
  • Sugar: A touch of white sugar balances the acidity of the tomatoes and enhances their natural sweetness.
  • Herbs and spices: Fresh parsley, dried basil, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper create a well-rounded, savory flavor profile.
  • Lasagna noodles: Use your favorite lasagna noodles, whether store-bought or homemade.
  • Cheese: A generous blend of Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta makes the dish extra rich and creamy.
  • Egg: Mixing an egg into the ricotta helps hold the cheese layer together when sliced.

How to Make Lasagna Step-By-Step

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

How to Layer Lasagna

Step 1: Spread 1 ½ cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.

Step 2: Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce, overlapping slightly.

Step 3: Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture.

Step 4: Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices.

Step 5: Spoon 1 ½ cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.

Step 6: Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
