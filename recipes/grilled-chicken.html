Grilled Chicken

from PxHere

Grilled chicken is juicy, smoky, and full of flavor. It's a versatile dish that pairs perfectly with salads, rice, or roasted vegetables. With the right marinade and technique, you'll have tender chicken with a beautiful char every time.

How To Make Grilled Chicken

You can follow this simple recipe to make juicy and flavorful grilled chicken at home.

Ingredients

  • Chicken: 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (or thighs).
  • Olive oil: 3 Tbsp.
  • Lemon juice: 2 Tbsp, freshly squeezed.
  • Garlic: 3 cloves, minced.
  • Herbs: 1 tsp dried oregano, 1 tsp dried thyme, 1 Tbsp fresh parsley (chopped).
  • Spices: 1 tsp paprika, ½ tsp black pepper, 1 tsp salt.

How to Make Grilled Chicken Step-By-Step

  1. Preheat & Prep
    • Heat grill to medium-high (about 200°C / 400°F).
    • Pat chicken dry with paper towels.
  2. Marinate the chicken
    • In a bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper.
    • Add chicken and coat well.
    • Cover and marinate for at least 30 minutes (up to 2 hours in the fridge for best flavor).
  3. Grill the chicken
    • Lightly oil the grill grates to prevent sticking.
    • Place chicken on the grill. Cook for 5-7 minutes per side, depending on thickness.
    • Chicken is done when it reaches an internal temperature of 75°C / 165°F and juices run clear.
  4. Rest & serve
    • Transfer chicken to a plate and let rest for 5 minutes to keep it juicy.
    • Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with lemon wedges.
